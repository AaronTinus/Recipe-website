Instructions

back to top
Steps
1 . Preheat the oven to 325 °F (160°). Grease one 8-inch cake pan with coconut oil. Cover the bottom and side of the pan with parchment paper and oil them too. Set aside.
2 . In a large mixing bowl, whisk dry ingredients: almond flour, sweetener, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
3 . In another bowl,whisk eggs with slightly cooled melted butter, water, and vanilla extract.
4 . Stir wet to dry ingredients until a smooth, slightly, thick batter forms.
5 . Pour the keto cake batter into the prepared pan and bake for 20-30 minutes in the center rack of the oven or until a pick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean or few a little crumb on it. Don't over bake or it gets dry.
6 . Cool down on a wire rack immediately until the cake reaches room temperature.
7 . Repeat the recipe for a two-layer cake, and frost the cake when both layers are at room temperature.
8 . Frost using one batch of Keto chacolate frosting.

Storage

back to top

Store cake in the fridge for up to 4 days or freeze and thaw in the fridge the day before.
