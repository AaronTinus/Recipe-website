Instructions

back to top
Steps
1 . Preheat the oven to 350 °
2 . Line the baking sheet, and use parchment paper.
3 . Mix cheese, almond flour, eggs, herbs, and spices in a big bowl until big roll forms and the ingredients are well blended.
4 . roll the dough into six rolls and set them on parchment paper. Rolls don't extend all that much but give them some space to do so.
5 . Melt butter in a small pot, then saute minced chives and garlic until fragrant, about 40 seconds.
6 . rolls should bake for ten minutes at 350 °
7 . Garlic butter should be applied to the rools after 15 minutes.
8 . For ten more minutes bake the rolls.
. Serve after quickly brushing more melted garlic butter on top!.

Storage

back to top

Store cake in the fridge for up to 4 days or freeze and thaw in the fridge the day before.
