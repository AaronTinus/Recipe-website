Instructions

Steps
1 . Preheat the oven to 350 °F
2 . Add 2 cups of mozzarella and crem cheese to a microwave safe bowl and microwave for 1 minute. Stir to combine and return to microwave until cheese has melted, about 30 more seconds.
3 . Stir in the Almond Flour, egg, and Italian seasoning to combine.
4 . Place the dough on a large sheet of parchment paper. Top with a second sheet of parchment.
5 . Roll the dough out into a 12 inch diameter circle.
6 . Remove the top piece of parchment and transfer the bottom sheet with the dough on it to a pizza pan.
7 . Bakefor ten minutes or until crust is lightly golden.
8 . For an extra sturdy crust that holds up well, carefully flip the crust over and bake for 3 more minutes.
9 . Remove crust from oven. Spread the marinara over the pizza crust and sprinkle with the remaining 1 1/2 cups mozzerella. Arrange pepperonievenly over the top.
10 . Bake the pizza for another 10 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before serving.
